The Approach

Gardens that are generous enough that everything thrives.

Gemma designs lush, structured landscapes where edible perennials, native plants, and pollinator habitat come together in something that feels abundant rather than manicured. The result is a garden that's beautiful to move through, alive with birds and wildlife, and generous enough that everything thrives — including you.
